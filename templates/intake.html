Symptom Timeline Builder

Take your time to describe what you've been experiencing. There are no wrong answers — the more detail you can give, the more useful your summary will be.

What does this tool do?

✓ What it does

  • Helps you organise your symptom history into a clear timeline
  • Creates a summary you can share with your doctor
  • Asks follow-up questions based on your answers

✕ What it does NOT do

  • Diagnose conditions
  • Recommend treatments or medication
  • Replace professional medical advice
  • Store your data after your session ends

You can write in any language.
